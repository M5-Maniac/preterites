⚠️ -CAR / -GAR / -ZAR — Spelling Change in YO form ONLY

-CAR → QUÉ  buscar → yo busqué  |  -GAR → GUÉ  pagar → yo pagué  |  -ZAR → CÉ  organizar → yo organicé

Other examples: llegar → llegué · practicar → practiqué · marcar → marqué · tocar → toqué · empezar → empecé · comenzar → comencé · explicar → expliqué · sacar → saqué

Stem Changers in the Preterite

📌 Stem Changer Rules

• -AR stem changers NEVER stem change in preterite (use regular -AR endings)
• -ER stem changers NEVER stem change in preterite (use regular -ER endings)
• -IR stem changers DO stem change — but ONLY in 3S and 3P:
   – e → i  (e.g. servir: sirvió, sirvieron)
   – o → u  (e.g. dormir: durmió, durmieron)
   – Use regular -IR endings everywhere else

⚠️ Colorful / J-U-I Verbs have their OWN rules — see the Colorful Verbs tab!

These verbs (DECIR, TRAER, HACER, TENER, ESTAR, PODER, PONER, SABER, QUERER, VENIR, ANDAR, CABER, CONDUCIR, PRODUCIR, TRADUCIR) use a completely different set of endings with no accent marks. Do not mix them with regular -AR/-ER/-IR verbs.

Colorful Verbs — J, U, I

These irregular verbs all share the same special endings — and have NO accent marks. Each has its own unique stem. The J verbs also drop the "i" in the ellos/ellas form.

⚠️ Key Reminders for Colorful Verbs

1. No accent marks anywhere — yo form is -e (not -é), él/ella form is -o (not -ó)
2. J verbs drop the "i" in ellos form → dijeron NOT dijieron
3. HACER hiZo — the c changes to z in él/ella ONLY to keep the soft sound
4. These verbs replace normal endings completely — the stem changes too
